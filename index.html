Your astonishing introduction of this picturesque family run boutique hotel can be summed in a few words." Home away from Home", centrally located- only 4 Km. from Indira Gandhi International Airport and about 16 Km. from the Delhi’s commercial centre of Rajiv Chowk and about 8kms away from the new business hub 'Gurgaon'.

Away from the concrete jungle of vertical buildings, at Ashok Country Resort you are checking into Nature, this Haven, is surrounded by the lush green lawns, the back drop of landscaped mounds against the azure, blue sky provide a mesmerizing view for the avid birdwatchers, the charismatic peacocks in their might splendour gracefully prancing among the vibrant hued flowers is a magnificent sight, the “Koyal” , our Indian bird singing melodiously from the tree top lulls you to sleep on the vast expanse of greens. This tranquil resort with unpolluted environs helps to rejuvenate body, mind and soul. The quiet poolside promises a breath taking view.
